Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Bangladesh has been commended
for its efforts in developing
an innovation ecosystem.

Sustainability

Economic reforms are at
the heart of the Bangladesh 2.0 vision.

Infrastructure

From tunnels and bridges to
a new deep-sea port, engineering
is transforming Bangladesh.

Empowerment

The process of becoming stronger and
more confident, especially in controlling
one's life and claiming one's rights.

The Vision of Future

The Bangladesh 2.0 agenda envisions a future where economic growth is not achieved at the expense of the environment but is instead driven by sustainable
practices that protect natural resources and ensure the well-being of future generations.

Digital Transformation

Digital infrastructure will become further embedded into our daily lives.

Green Energy

Bangladesh is increasingly focusing on renewable energy and energy efficiency.

Modern Infrastructure

Modern Infrastructure in Bangladesh 2.0 accelerates development with innovation.
